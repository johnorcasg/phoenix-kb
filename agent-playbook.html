Agent Playbook – LOI & Terms Flexibility

Version 1.0 • Updated 2025-09-12 • Phoenix Inbound KB

Objectives

  • Role & status (listing/buyer; active/pending/off-market).
  • Ask price & firmness; seller priorities (speed vs price vs monthly).
  • Openness to Subto/Wrap/Hybrid/Cash/Carry.
  • Collect LOI email; preferred outline.

Positioning

“We align structure with seller priorities—timing, net, or monthly. I’ll note flexibility and send an outline.”

LOI Contents (typical)

  • Structure, range/framework, timeline, access, DD, closing costs, assignment (if allowed), leaseback/post-occ, next-step call window.

Negotiation patterns

  • Price pressure → offer terms to preserve price.
  • Terms pressure → offer speed/certainty for price modesty.
  • Stale listing → Hybrid/Carry bridge affordability.
  • Multiple offers → speed/clean terms; propose backup if UC.

Routing

  • Tag strategy openness; stay in Negotiation or Follow-Up Pending as fit.

Operational knowledge only. No guarantees, no legal/tax advice.
